About

Hi! Thanks for visiting. This website essentially is my way of expressing my admiration for good quality stationery and blabbing about it to whoever will listen.

From glitter pens to cute sticky note sets, I have always loved stationery. So, I thought I would share this passion with others through silly informal blog posts.

For me, this site is mainly a personal project grounded in personal experience and a curiosity about the stationery products that will be around in the future. For you, it may be something you've discovered by accident or even an opportunity to learn about products and brands you've never tried before.

You might not even like stationery to begin with (and that's cool!). Whatever the reason, I hope you'll enjoy your time here nonetheless.
